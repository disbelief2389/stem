# stem
>"StemHub"

A git/GitHub parallel for open-source music production.
## Mindmap Excerpt
>Refer to the mindmap resources for better understanding.
### What?
- A git/GitHub parallel for music production
- The foundation for an open-source approach to music production 
- A proposal for new methods
- Cloud-based version control
- Inevitable
- Logical
- Mass collaboration
- Music projects without any centralised ownership
- Necessary
- Possible
- Revocation of monetary incentive

### When?
- Now
- The future
- Yesterday
- (literally a timeless idea)

### Who?
- Helps to lower the bar for newer producers
- Honest musicians and artists
- Threatens music contractors' $$$

### How?
- Brainstorming
- Critical and explicit discussions
- Inspire others
- The ripest area for discussion
- Radical self-reliance and defiance
- Work with software developers

### Where?
- Anywhere and everywhere
- Online and in person

### Why?
- Counterbalance to financial incentives
- Extreme bias towards "music rights" or "music ownership."
- Extreme demand
- Far too many producers gatekeep at the drop of a hat
- The industrialisation of music threatens its future
